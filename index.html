Step 2: Create your own version

Step 3: Redirect the clicks

From there, you point that borrowed attention at any link you want — an affiliate offer, a Skool group, a digital product, or an AI tool. Because the underlying content is already proven to convert attention into clicks, the heavy lifting is done before you ever publish.
